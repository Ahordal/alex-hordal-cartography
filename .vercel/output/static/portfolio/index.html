Portfolio

A collection of cartography, GIS, and data visualization projects focused on terrain, spatial analysis, and visual communication.

Interactive GIS

World Ports

An interactive web mapping application developed with Mapbox GL JS and Mapbox Studio, showcasing major ports worldwide through custom vector tiles, responsive popup interactions, and scale-dependent cartographic design.

Nova Scotia Mine Openings

An interactive web mapping application built using the ArcGIS Maps SDK for JavaScript, visualizing 8,565 abandoned mine openings across Nova Scotia through clustering, GeoJSON integration, and interactive feature exploration.

The Colours of Sweden

A responsive SVG-based thematic web map exploring Swedish provincial heraldry through the ai2html Adobe Illustrator plugin, interactive vector graphics, semantic HTML/CSS structure, and custom DOM-based interaction.

Data Visualization

NYC: Dining Under the Microscope

An interactive ArcGIS Online dashboard project exploring restaurant inspection patterns and food safety trends across New York City through dynamic filtering, linked inspection reports, and restaurant performance tracking over time.

NYC: Yellow Taxi Trip Records

A spatial–temporal visualization project using ArcGIS Pro to animate 24 hours of New York City yellow taxi trip activity.

Convective Storms

A statistical cartographic poster visualizing the spatial distribution and impacts of severe convective storm activity across the contiguous United States between 2014 and 2023 using NOAA Storm Events Database records.

NTSB: 40 Years of Aviation Investigations

A Visual Analysis of 91,854 NTSB Aviation Investigations Worldwide (1985–2024)

Cartographic Design

The Colour of Maps

A cartographic poster comparing three themed versions of the same park map to show how colour, typography, and visual style can change the look and feel of a map.

ULLR Maps

A terrain-focused georeferenced ski map of Whistler Blackcomb designed to improve spatial awareness, terrain readability, and mountain navigation across the largest ski resort in North America.

Hawaiʻi: Seafloor to Summit

A topobathymetric map of the main eight Hawaiian Islands exploring their full vertical extent from seafloor to summit.

A Comparative Look at Map Projections

A large-format cartographic poster exploring projection distortion, spatial properties, and regional mapping applications across six coordinate reference systems.

Zion Terrain Atlas

A Zion National Park Landform and Terrain Atlas, designed in ArcGIS Pro.

Fourchu Coast Wilderness Paddling Map

A large-format recreational paddling map of the Fourchu Coast Wilderness Area in Cape Breton, Nova Scotia, developed in collaboration with Canoe Kayak Nova Scotia using ArcGIS Pro and Adobe Illustrator.

Multiscale Roadmap Taxonomy

A multiscale roadmap taxonomy project focused on cartographic hierarchy, scale-dependent symbology, and progressive detail across zoom levels.

Terrain Atlas Book Production

A multi-page relief atlas integrating advanced terrain visualization techniques with professional publication and print production standards.

Spatial Analysis

Dalhousie Solar Panel Analysis

A GIS-based solar insolation analysis using LiDAR-derived elevation data to evaluate rooftop solar potential across selected buildings on the Dalhousie University campus.

QGIS Spatial Visualization Series

A series of spatial visualizations developed in QGIS exploring earthquake occurrence patterns across the contiguous United States using heatmaps, hotspot analysis, and hexbin aggregation.

Incident Response

A four-map emergency response series designed in ArcGIS Pro to support a fictional train derailment scenario in the Halifax Regional Municipality.

Terrain & Relief Visualization

Obqlique Panorama

An oblique panorama of the Grand Canyon integrating digital elevation, orthophotography, and cartographic refinement workflows.

Terrain Labs

A series of 7 terrain visualization labs exploring advanced relief representation techniques using DEMs, LiDAR, hillshading, and 3D terrain modelling workflows.
